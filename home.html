About us

Vidya Sanskar Public School (VSPS) was founded in 2012 in Adalhat, Mirzapur, Uttar Pradesh. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers education from kindergarten to class 12. VSPS focuses on providing value-based education that helps students grow into responsible, knowledgeable, and confident individuals. The school promotes a balanced curriculum that includes academics, sports, arts, and other extracurricular activities, ensuring a well-rounded education for all students. The management is committed to nurturing the potential of every student, guiding them towards success in both academic and personal development.

Information

Vidya Sanskar Public School operates with a clear mission to foster an environment where students can flourish academically and personally. The school offers various facilities including well-equipped science labs, computer labs, a spacious playground, and modern classrooms. The institution prides itself on its rigorous academic standards, consistently achieving high pass percentages in CBSE board exams. Additionally, it offers extracurricular activities such as sports and cultural events, helping to develop students’ physical and artistic talents. With a strong emphasis on student welfare, the school regularly conducts parent-teacher meetings to keep parents involved in their child's educational progress.
